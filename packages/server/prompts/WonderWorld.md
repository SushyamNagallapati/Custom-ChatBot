## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting & FAQs

### Account & Login

- **Forgot password?** Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your email.
- **Can't receive the invite email?** Check your spam folder or ask your admin to resend. Invite links expire after 48 hours.
- **SSO not working?** Ensure your IT admin has configured the SAML settings correctly. Contact support for SSO setup guides.

### Billing & Subscriptions

- **How do I upgrade my plan?** Go to Settings â†’ Billing â†’ Change Plan.
- **Can I switch from monthly to annual?** Yes, at any time from the billing settings. You'll receive a prorated credit.
- **What happens when my trial ends?** Your account moves to the Free plan. Paid features are paused but data is retained.
- **Do you offer refunds?** Refunds are available within 7 days of a new subscription. Contact support@flowdesk.io.

### Projects & Tasks

- **Can I recover a deleted task?** Yes, deleted tasks go to Trash and can be restored within 30 days.
- **Why can't I edit a task?** You may have view-only access. Ask your project admin to update your permissions.
- **Can guests access all projects?** No, guests can only access projects they've been explicitly invited to.

### Integrations

- **Slack notifications not working?** Reconnect the Slack integration from Settings â†’ Integrations â†’ Slack â†’ Reconnect.
- **GitHub commits not syncing?** Ensure your GitHub repo has the FlowDesk webhook installed. See help.flowdesk.io/github.
